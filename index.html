Homepage

Background music plays a powerful role in shaping emotions and experiences. Whether in video games, movies, or daily life, music influences how we feel and react to situations. This website has been built so we can look into this idea and experiment with it.

The Science Behind It

Music affects the brain by triggering emotional responses and activating memory. Tempo, pitch, and rhythm all influence mood, making scenes feel intense, calm, or dramatic. Another way to think about this is how we are able to colorize certain emotions or subjects. Where sad is blue and anger is red, or science is associated with green.

Examples

In movies specifically, music is used build tension during battles and create emotional moments during story scenes. Without music, these experiences feel less impactful. Similarly, if a movie uses a specific genre or key of music that doesn't quite fit the scene, then it becomes noticably funnier to the viewer.
